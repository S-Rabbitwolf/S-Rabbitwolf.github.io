Utilization of HTML Semantic Elements

Semantic arguments in terms of HTML prompts are in the name. The nature of semantic arguments denotes that they define the content of what it entails. For example, the img tag is semantic due to its use as an identifier for images on a page. Non-semantic arguments, like div, h(x) etc. do not denote specific content. There are two main benefits to utilizing semantic tags. The first is that you are able to much more easily identify information you notate in your code.
