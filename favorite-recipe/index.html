Tocino Time!

Cured meat, Filipino style! My apologies to every vegan out there. I respect your tenacity, for I lack it.

What You'll Need:

  • 1-2 lbs pork (preferably flank meat)
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup fruit juice (again, optional)
  • 1/2 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp rice flour (to give it that little extra texture!)

Instructions:

  1. Combine every non-pork ingredient into an appropriately sized mixing bowl. Make sure everything is blended to your liking before moving on!
  2. Add the pork and hand-mix it. It's important to hand-mix it to ensure the meat isn't crushed or damaged. Use gloves, kids!
  3. Transfer it to a container with a cover, letting it sit overnight unrefrigerated.
