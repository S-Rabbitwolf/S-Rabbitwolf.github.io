"Eons ago, a cataclysmic event shook existence to its core, reducing all of reality into a single point. All of creation, or perhaps what was left of it, coalesced into this primordial essence. In time, this essence would develop into a formless will, which was not unlike a child's first words. This will, aware of its own ignorance, decided it needed to learn more. Having naught but faint memories of countless lives unlived, the will, now calling itself Eid, came to understand its own limitless potential amidst the silent screams of the void. Eid soon realized that it was alone. Despite its unbridled power, it was unable to create anything with such limited space. So Eid, accepting its loneliness, willed itself away, succumbing its knowledge to nothingness. In that nothingness, the seed of knowledge bloomed into the very heart of existence. And thus, the Eidleik came to be." - Scripture 1:5 - Solitude
